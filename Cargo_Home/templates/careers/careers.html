Logistics Coordinator

Location: Kochi Experience: 1+ years

Responsibilities

Coordinate and monitor shipments from origin to destination. Communicate with carriers, suppliers, and customers to ensure timely delivery of goods. Manage documentation and customs clearance processes. Resolve any shipping-related issues or delays. Optimize logistics operations to improve efficiency and reduce costs.

Requirements

Bachelor's degree in logistics, supply chain management, or a related field. Proven experience in logistics coordination or freight forwarding. Strong communication and problem-solving skills. Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and prioritize tasks effectively. Knowledge of international shipping regulations and procedures.
