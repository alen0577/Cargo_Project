Book a Pickup

Customers can initiate a shipping request through the website. They provide necessary details such as pickup and delivery addresses, package weight, and desired delivery speed.

Shipping Availability Check

The system checks the availability of shipping services in the specified pickup and delivery locations. If shipping is available, the process continues; otherwise, the customer is informed.

Home Pickup Process/Drop-off at Shipping Center

If home pickup is chosen, the system schedules a pickup with a designated courier. Alternatively, customers can choose to drop off their package at a nearby shipping center.

Product Verification & Weight Calculation

Upon receiving the package, the courier verifies its contents and weighs it to calculate shipping charges.

Delivery to Receiver

Customers provide sender and receiver details. The system generates a unique tracking code for the shipment. The package is transported to the designated shipping center closest to the receiver's address. Once delivered, an executive updates the system with the delivery status.
